About Don Boos

Don Boos was born on Long Island in 1985. He attended Smithtown High School where he expressed a strong affinity for art and weird things associated with it. Later he attended Rochester Insitute of Technology where he majored in photography.

After graduation from RIT he spent some time with Greenpeace before moving to Denver. While in Denver he developed an interest in tattoos as an outlet for his artistic expression. Don went to every tattoo parlor he could find in search of an apprenticeship. After completing his apprenticeship in Denver he moved back to Long Island to start his career in earnest. He has been tattoing now for over 5 years.
